Revolutionary Features

Combining AI intelligence with serverless excellence

Intelligent AI Selection

Sophisticated worthiness algorithm analyzes content quality, session duration, and emotional journey to make cost-effective AI enhancement decisions. Reduces AI costs by 60% while ensuring valuable sessions get premium treatment.

Event-Driven Serverless

6 specialized Lambda functions orchestrated by Step Functions and EventBridge Pipes. Auto-scaling architecture handles 1000+ concurrent users with sub-100ms response times and minimal infrastructure costs.

Emotion-Aware Enhancement

AI understands emotional journeys and creates personalized content reflecting your transformation from 'focused' to 'accomplished'. Promotes healthy productivity patterns with reward systems.

Multi-Region Support

Tested in eu-west-3 with automatic model fallback. Region-aware Bedrock integration supports Nova Lite in US/EU/APAC regions with Claude Haiku as universal backup.

Cost Intelligence

Smart budget tracking with automatic rewards. Users earn AI credits for consistent engagement, breakthrough sessions, and positive outcomes. Built-in freemium model for commercial viability.

Production Ready

Comprehensive error handling, structured logging with PowerTools, monitoring, and sophisticated retry logic. TTL-based data lifecycle management reduces storage costs by 80%.
